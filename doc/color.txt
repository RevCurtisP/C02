//C02 Color Functions
//Prototype - Subject to Change

  txtclr(c);          Sets text color to system color c. 
            
                      Note: May call system routine or may print color change
                      sequence.

  bkgclr(c);          Sets background color to system color c.
                      
                      Note: May call system routine or may print color change
                      sequence.
